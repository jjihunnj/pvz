Plants vs. Zombies 2 (formerly known as Plants vs. Zombies 2: It's About Time) is a 2013 free tower defense video game developed by PopCap Games and published by Electronic Arts. It is the sequel to Plants vs. Zombies, and was released worldwide on Apple App Store on August 15, 2013, and Google Play on October 23, 2013. The player defends the lawn by placing a variety of plants. The player must battle the zombies in different time periods, including Ancient Egypt, the 16th and 17th centuries, Ice age, the Far Future, the Middle Ages, and the 1980s.(I took information from wikipedia)
